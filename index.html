Sustainability and Eco-friendly Fabric

Besides our focus on two major synthetic fabrics, Polyester and Nylon, we are continually expanding our integrated supply chains to develop a more diverse range of high-performance fabrics in both Function and Technology.

This endeavor enables us to add significant value across various apparel sectors, including fashion, casual wear, sportswear, and uniforms, meeting the diverse demands for convenient and versatile garments in our daily lives.

Moreover, our commitment extends to utilizing environmentally friendly fibers, incorporating Plant-derived and Recycled materials. By embracing sustainable practices, we aim to not only enhance our product offerings but also contribute positively to the environment.
